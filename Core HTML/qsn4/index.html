List Of Tags:

  1. h1: Defines a heading of level 1
  2. p:Defines a paragraph of text.
  3. div:Defines a container
  4. from :creates a section for form
  5. table: Define a table with rows and columns.
  6. ul:Defines unordered list.
  7. ol:defines ordered list.
  8. br: breaks from the line and go to next line.
  9. hr:creates a horizontal rule.
